Nagpur: Metro rides are getting longer and reaching farther—Nagpur Metro is making serious progress in 2026! With Phase 2 piers nearly half done (684 out of 1,514 completed as of late January), viaducts rising fast on key corridors, and the existing Orange and Aqua Lines expanding their reach, the city's public transport game is leveling up.

## Current Operational Routes (2026)

### Orange Line (North-South Corridor)
**Route:** Khapri - Sitabuldi - Airport South

- **Length:** 19 km
- **Stations:** 17
- **Operational Since:** March 2019
- **Daily Ridership:** ~35,000 passengers
- **Frequency:** Every 7-10 minutes during peak hours

**Key Stations:**
- Khapri (Integrated Bus Terminal)
- Automotive Square
- New Airport
- Sitabuldi Interchange Station
- Airport South

### Aqua Line (East-West Corridor)
**Route:** Lokmanya Nagar - Sitabuldi - Prajapati Nagar

- **Length:** 13.5 km
- **Stations:** 13
- **Operational Since:** December 2019
- **Daily Ridership:** ~30,000 passengers

**Key Stations:**
- Lokmanya Nagar
- Ambazari
- Kasturchand Park
- Sitabuldi Interchange Station
- Congress Nagar
- Prajapati Nagar

## How to Use Nagpur Metro (For First-Timers)

1. **Buy Token/Card** at station counter or kiosk
2. **Scan** at entry gate
3. **Check** route map and platform number
4. **Board** the correct line (Orange/Aqua)
5. **Exit** by scanning token at destination gate

**Pro Tips:**
- Download Nagpur Metro Rail app for schedules
- Avoid rush hours (8-10 AM, 5-7 PM) if possible
- Stand on the right on escalators
- Keep belongings secure
- No eating/drinking inside coaches
